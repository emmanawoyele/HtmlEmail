    • Pieces suitable for party painting are items such as plates, cups, tiles and mugs which allow for a picture to be painted onto the pottery. The host can arrange for a choice of two or three pieces to be offered and the children choose their picture from our selection, or painters can draw their own if preferred. The pottery is left with us to be glazed and fired and will be ready for collection from the studio within a week - individually wrapped and named. All paint is washable and non toxic.
